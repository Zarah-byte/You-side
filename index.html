About YOU·SIDE
Music says everything words can't

Music says everything words can't. The songs you keep on repeat, the albums that defined your summers, the tracks you play at 2am — they're not just sounds, they're a mirror.

Our platform takes the music you love and transforms it into something deeply personal: a curated album crafted just for you, alongside a personality analysis that uncovers what your listening habits reveal about who you are.

Whether you're drawn to melancholic indie ballads or high-energy anthems, every song you input becomes a data point in a bigger story — your story. Discover the soundtrack of your soul, and maybe learn a little something about yourself along the way.
